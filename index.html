About Us

The Sewa foundation started with a mission to act against the Global warming and save the environment by planting trees across India and educate the student and try to eradicate poverty. Till now we have collaborated with over 10 Lakh people to plant more than 1 crore trees across India. We have been successful in renovating the local Government school with the help of the contributions made by our members.
Our goal is to expand our successful model that we pioneered in India and expand it globally to improve living standards, reduce global warming and save and plant more and more trees every year.
